OLIVIA AHERNE IS AN INDEPENDENT CURATOR BASED IN LONDON. SHE HOLDS AN MA IN CURATING CONTEMPORARY ART FROM THE ROYAL COLLEGE OF ART, LONDON AND HAS CURATED PROJECTS IN PARTNERSHIP WITH LUX, FOR LEWISHAM ARTHOUSE AND AT THE ONASSIS CULTURAL CENTRE IN ATHENS, GREECE. SHE IS ALSO THE CO-FOUNDER AND CURATOR OF DRIVE-THRU AND HAS CONTRIBUTED TO PUBLICATIONS SUCH AS THISISTOMORROW AND ORLANDO.
